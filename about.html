OUR BEGINNING

From Vision to Movement

IBD was born from a passion for authentic street culture and contemporary design.

Founded in 2020 by Dennis, IBD Streetwear emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: streetwear should be more than just clothing—it should be a form of self-expression, a statement of individuality, and a celebration of urban culture.

What started as a small collection of hand-printed t-shirts in a Brooklyn apartment has grown into a globally recognized brand, worn by artists, musicians, and style enthusiasts who value authenticity and quality.

Dennis' vision was to bridge the gap between high-fashion aesthetics and accessible streetwear, creating pieces that tell stories and inspire confidence. Every IBD garment is designed with intention, crafted for longevity, and infused with the spirit of the streets that inspired it.

Today, IBD continues to push boundaries while staying true to its roots—creating collections that honor the past while looking firmly toward the future of street culture.
